                Quick fixes

                • No recording? Use “Upload existing video” and check camera permissions.
                • Uploads fail? Verify API base and rate limits; mock URLs don’t upload.
                • No notifications? Tap “Enable notifications” and keep this tab open.
                • Low bandwidth? Lower bitrate and duration; watch the estimated size.
